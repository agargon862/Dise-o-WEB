MORA

Singer · Songwriter · Producer

BIOGRAPHY

From San Juan, Puerto Rico, Mora stands out as a creative force shaping the future of Latin urban music. Mixing reggaetón, trap, and experimental sounds, he turns emotion into rhythm and style into identity.

Recognized for his melodic vision and fearless approach, Mora blends introspection with innovation—crafting music that feels both fresh and timeless.

The voice of a generation that moves, thinks, and dreams differently.

THE origins

Long before the stages and studios, Mora was a curious kid who found music in everything around him.

At home in Puerto Rico, everyday sounds turned into melodies, and creativity became his way to understand the world.

Between drawings, games, and early rhythms, he began to imagine what would later become his life — making others feel through sound.

From those moments, his way of creating was born: guided by intuition, emotion, and a genuine connection to what’s real.

That essence is still there —

the same kid, still experimenting, just with a much bigger stage.

THE AESTHETICS

The Versatile Look and Identity


Mora’s image has evolved from raw, urban streetwear to a modern sophistication, effortlessly blending high-fashion with essential accessories. He is a master of layering, using jewelry, statement sunglasses, and oversized garments that act as an extension of his sound. This versatile identity projects a cool, controlled seriousness that contrasts perfectly with the raw emotion of his lyrics.
